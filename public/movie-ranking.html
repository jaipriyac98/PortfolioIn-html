The Best Movies According to Jaipriya

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

Why it’s great: A powerful story of hope, resilience, and friendship set against the backdrop of prison life.

Inception (2010)

Why it’s great: A mind-bending film by Christopher Nolan that explores dreams within dreams with stunning visuals and a gripping plot.

Parasite (2019)

Why it’s great: A masterpiece from South Korea that delves into class disparities with suspense, humor, and unexpected twists..
